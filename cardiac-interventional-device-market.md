<p><p>The cardiac interventional device market encompasses various types, primarily including coronary stents, angioplasty balloons, and catheters. Coronary stents are mesh-like tubes inserted into arteries to maintain blood flow. Angioplasty balloons are inflated within narrowed arteries to improve circulation. Catheters are flexible tubes used for diagnostic or therapeutic procedures, often facilitating the delivery of other devices or medications. Other devices in this market may include guidewires and embolic protection devices, supporting a range of cardiac interventions for improved patient outcomes.</p></p>
