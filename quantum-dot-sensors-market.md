<p><ul><li>Resistance Strain Quantum Dot Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Quantum Dot Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum dot sensors utilize semiconductor nanocrystals to enhance sensitivity and functionality in various applications. Resistance strain quantum dot sensors measure changes in resistance due to mechanical strain, offering high precision in detecting deformations. Piezoresistive quantum dot sensors, on the other hand, exploit the piezoresistive effect, where electrical resistance changes under applied stress, making them ideal for pressure and force measurement. Both types are pivotal in industries like automotive, aerospace, and healthcare, providing advanced sensing capabilities and improving performance in smart technologies.</p></p>
