<p><p>Self Sovereign Identity (SSI) markets can be categorized into three main types based on blockchain types. </p><p>1. **Public Blockchain**: These are decentralized and open to everyone, providing a transparent and secure identity framework for users to control their own data. </p><p>2. **Private Blockchain**: Restricted to authorized participants, these systems allow organizations to manage identities while ensuring privacy and compliance with regulations. </p><p>3. **Consortium Blockchain**: A hybrid approach where a group of entities collaborates to govern the blockchain, balancing control and transparency for shared identity management.</p></p>
