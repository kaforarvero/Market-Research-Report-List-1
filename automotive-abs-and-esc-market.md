Four Channel ABS provides individual control of each wheel, enhancing braking efficacy in modern vehicles. ESC systems, which work in tandem with ABS, utilize wheel speed sensors to prevent skidding and maintain vehicle stability by selectively applying brakes, ensuring improved safety and control.</p></p>
