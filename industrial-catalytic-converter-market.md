<p><p>The industrial catalytic converter market consists of two primary types: two-way and three-way converters. Two-way converters focus on reducing carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, making them suitable for simpler engine systems. In contrast, three-way converters are designed to optimize the reduction of nitrogen oxides, hydrocarbons, and carbon monoxide simultaneously, which enhances overall emissions control. The choice between these types depends on the specific emissions regulations and technological requirements of the industrial applications, ensuring compliance and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
