<p><p>Projection mapping equipment primarily consists of two main types: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCD (Liquid Crystal Display). DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors to create bright, sharp images, making them ideal for large-scale projections and dynamic visuals. Conversely, LCD projectors employ liquid crystal panels to produce vibrant colors and excellent detail, making them suitable for environments requiring accurate color reproduction. Both types of projectors serve diverse applications in events, art installations, and advertising within the projection mapping market.</p></p>
