<p><strong>The Semiconductor Thermal Evaporators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Effect Evaporation</li><li>Multi Effect Evaporation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor thermal evaporators market is categorized into two main types: single effect evaporation and multi effect evaporation. Single effect evaporation involves a straightforward process where a solvent is evaporated in a single stage, making it simpler and cost-effective for smaller production needs. In contrast, multi effect evaporation utilizes multiple stages to recover energy from the evaporated solvent, enhancing efficiency and reducing operational costs, making it more suitable for large-scale semiconductor fabrication processes.</p></p>
