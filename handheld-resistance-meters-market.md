<p><p>The handheld resistance meters market is segmented into low resistance testing and large resistance testing. Low resistance testing devices measure resistances typically below 1 ohm, essential for applications like electrical connectors and motor windings, ensuring optimal conductivity. In contrast, large resistance testing meters, which assess resistances in the megaohm range, are used for applications such as insulation testing in power systems and high-voltage equipment. Both segments cater to various industries, emphasizing safety and reliability in electrical systems.</p></p>
