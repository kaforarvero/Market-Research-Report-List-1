<p><p>The forklift tires market features three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic forklift tires are inflatable, providing excellent shock absorption and traction for outdoor use. Solid forklift tires, made from resilient rubber, offer durability and resistance to punctures, making them ideal for indoor applications and rough surfaces. Polyurethane forklift tires are lightweight and provide low rolling resistance, enhancing energy efficiency and minimizing wear on floors, making them suitable for manufacturing and warehousing environments. Each type serves distinct operational needs.</p></p>
