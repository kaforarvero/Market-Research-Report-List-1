<p><p>The backup camera modules market primarily features two types of imaging technologies: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) cameras and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras are known for their superior image quality and low-light performance, making them ideal for high-resolution backup systems. In contrast, CMOS cameras are more power-efficient, cost-effective, and capable of integrating additional features, such as advanced image processing. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in automotive safety, enhancing visibility while reversing vehicles.</p></p>
