Circular staplers are designed for joining tissue ends in procedures such as gastrointestinal surgeries, offering precise, consistent closure in a circular manner. Linear staplers, on the other hand, are used to create straight lines of staples for a variety of surgical applications, including thoracic and abdominal surgeries, providing efficient tissue approximation. Both types enhance surgical efficiency, reduce operating time, and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
