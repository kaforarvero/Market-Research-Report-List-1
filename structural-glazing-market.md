<p><p>The structural glazing market includes various types of glass, each serving unique functional and aesthetic purposes. Insulating glass consists of two or more panes separated by a spacer, enhancing thermal efficiency. Tempered glass undergoes heat treatment for increased strength and safety, making it ideal for high-stress environments. Low-e glass features a special coating that reflects infrared light, improving energy efficiency. The "Other" category encompasses specialized glass options not fitting the main types, such as laminated or decorative glass, catering to diverse architectural needs.</p></p>
