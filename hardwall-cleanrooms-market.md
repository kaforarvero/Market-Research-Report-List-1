<p><strong>The Hardwall Cleanrooms Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Cleanroom</li><li>Negative Pressure Cleanroom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardwall cleanroom market is categorized into positive pressure and negative pressure cleanrooms. Positive pressure cleanrooms maintain an air pressure that is higher than the surrounding environment, preventing contaminants from entering, making them ideal for manufacturing pharmaceuticals and semiconductors. In contrast, negative pressure cleanrooms have lower pressure, containing potential contaminants within, thus safeguarding sensitive processes, particularly in healthcare and research settings. Both types are essential for controlled environments, ensuring product safety and compliance with strict regulatory standards.</p></p>
