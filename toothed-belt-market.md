<p><ul><li>One Side</li><li>Double Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toothed belt market is categorized into two main types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided toothed belts have teeth on only one side, offering efficient power transmission and space-saving benefits, making them ideal for applications with limited installation space. In contrast, double-sided toothed belts feature teeth on both sides, allowing for smoother operation and the flexibility to run in multiple directions. This versatility makes them suitable for more complex machinery and applications requiring simultaneous power transfer from both sides.</p></p>
