<p><ul><li>Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL)</li><li>Ultrasonic Test (UT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent pigging services involve using specialized tools, known as "pigs," to inspect pipelines for integrity and corrosion. The Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) method employs magnetic fields to detect flaws, making it effective for metallic pipelines. In contrast, Ultrasonic Testing (UT) utilizes high-frequency sound waves to identify defects and measure wall thickness, offering precise assessments for various pipe materials. Both methods are crucial for maintaining pipeline safety and efficiency, catering to industries such as oil and gas, water distribution, and more.</p></p>
