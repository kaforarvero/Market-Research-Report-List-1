<p><p>The Hepatitis C Virus (HCV) testing market includes several key types. HCV antibody tests detect antibodies produced in response to the virus, indicating past or current infection. HCV viral load tests measure the amount of virus in the bloodstream, essential for assessing disease progression and treatment efficacy. HCV genotyping tests identify the specific strain of the virus, guiding treatment decisions. Together, these testing options facilitate accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and monitoring of HCV-infected patients.</p></p>
