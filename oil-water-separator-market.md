<p><ul><li>Gravity OWS</li><li>Centrifugal OWS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil-water separator market comprises two primary types: gravity-based and centrifugal separators. Gravity OWS utilizes differences in density to separate oil from water, allowing oil to float to the surface for removal. This method is commonly used in various industries for its simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, centrifugal OWS employs centrifugal force to enhance separation efficiency, making it suitable for applications requiring rapid processing and higher purity levels. Both types are essential for environmental management and compliance.</p></p>
