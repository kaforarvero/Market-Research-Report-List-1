<p><p>Data Center Interconnect (DCI) solutions can be categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based DCI utilizes third-party cloud services to connect data centers, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced upfront costs. This model is ideal for businesses seeking rapid deployment and access to advanced features without heavy investment in infrastructure. Conversely, on-premises DCI involves deploying dedicated hardware and infrastructure within the organization’s facilities, providing greater control, security, and customization but often at a higher initial cost.</p></p>
