<p><p>The residential air purification equipment market includes various types designed to improve indoor air quality. High-Efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters capture tiny particles like dust and allergens. Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs) use electrical charges to remove particulate matter from the air. Ionizers release charged particles that attach to pollutants, causing them to settle. Ozone Generators create ozone to eliminate odors and contaminants, although their safety and effectiveness can be contentious. Each type serves specific air cleaning functions suited to different needs.</p></p>
