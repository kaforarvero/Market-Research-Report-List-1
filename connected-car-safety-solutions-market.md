<p><p>The Connected Car Safety Solutions market encompasses various types of vehicle communication systems. Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I) facilitates communication between vehicles and roadway systems for improved traffic management. Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V) enables direct communication between cars to enhance safety through collision warnings. Vehicle-to-Cloud (V2C) connects vehicles to cloud services for data analysis and updates. Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P) aims to protect pedestrians by alerting them to nearby vehicles. Vehicle-to-Everything (V2X) integrates all these forms, enhancing overall transportation safety and efficiency.</p></p>
