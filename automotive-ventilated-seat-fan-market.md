<p><strong>The Automotive Ventilated Seat Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Radial Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ventilated seat fan market primarily consists of axial and radial fans. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan’s axis, providing effective airflow and energy efficiency, ideal for maintaining comfort in vehicle seats. Radial fans, on the other hand, propel air perpendicular to the axis, generating higher pressure and airflow suitable for larger spaces or concentrated cooling areas. Both fan types enhance passenger comfort by improving air circulation and temperature regulation in automotive seating systems.</p></p>
