<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG antibody rapid test kits market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **RDT (Rapid Diagnostic Test)** offers quick results, detecting IgM and IgG antibodies using simple procedures, ideal for immediate screening.</p><p>2. **ELISA (Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay)** is a laboratory-based method that provides more sensitive and specific results, often used for large-scale testing.</p><p>3. **Neutralization Assay** evaluates the functional ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, important for determining immune response strength. Each type serves distinct purposes in diagnosis and research.</p></p>
