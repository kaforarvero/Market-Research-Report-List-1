<p><p>The auxiliaries for the fertilizers market includes various agents that enhance the effectiveness and application of fertilizers. Emulsifiers facilitate the blending of oil and water-based components, improving consistency. Fixing agents enhance nutrient retention in soil, promoting efficiency. Dispersing agents ensure even distribution of fertilizers in soil, preventing clumping. Defoamers reduce foam during application, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste. Other auxiliary substances may include stabilizers and moisture retainers, further optimizing fertilizer performance and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
