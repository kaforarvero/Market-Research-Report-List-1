<p><strong>The Lipoprotein A Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochromatography</li><li>Turbidimetric Inhibition Immuno Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Lipoprotein A Test Kit market is primarily segmented into two types: Immunochromatography and Turbidimetric Inhibition Immunoassay. Immunochromatography utilizes a simple, rapid test format that detects Lipoprotein A levels through a visual change on a test strip, making it user-friendly and suitable for point-of-care testing. In contrast, Turbidimetric Inhibition Immunoassay quantifies Lipoprotein A by measuring the turbidity change in a sample after antigen-antibody interaction, providing high sensitivity and specificity in clinical laboratories. Both types facilitate cardiovascular risk assessment.</p></p>
