<p><strong>The Industrial Detonator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prompt Detonator</li><li>Delay Detonator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial detonator market is primarily divided into two types: prompt detonators and delay detonators. Prompt detonators initiate explosives instantaneously upon activation, making them ideal for immediate blasting applications in mining and construction. Delay detonators, on the other hand, provide a timed ignition, allowing for sequential blasting. This is advantageous for controlling shock waves and reducing unwanted ground movement, enhancing safety and efficiency in operations. Both types are essential in various industries, including mining, construction, and quarrying.</p></p>
