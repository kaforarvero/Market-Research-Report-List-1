<p><p>The Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBCs) market is segmented into four main types based on their construction and safety features. Type A FIBCs are general-purpose and not designed for hazardous materials. Type B FIBCs offer limited anti-static properties for low-risk applications. Type C FIBCs are conductive and are used in environments with explosive dust, requiring grounding. Finally, Type D FIBCs are designed to be inherently anti-static, suitable for flammable materials without the need for grounding. Each type serves specific industry safety needs.</p></p>
